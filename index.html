Celebrating Safely

In the interest of holding a gathering where all feel welcome and safe, we are requiring all guests be vaccinated against COVID and remain up to date on their boosters. Though the worst of the pandemic appears to be behind us, our venue will be strictly adhering to the latest public health guidance. We encourage guests to contact us prior to the event if they have concerns. Click to read our full statement on COVID considerations.

Weddings have social media policies now? We must be living in the future 🙄. We invite guests to take pictures, record videos, and capture any moment they please from our special day! Help us keep track of any social media posts from the wedding by including the hashtag #DavidAndAdrienneWed or #ShesASchulman.


The only time we ask you to be mindful of your phone is during the ceremony. We humbly request that if you do take a photo or record a video, it is done without use of a flash.
